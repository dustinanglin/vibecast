Vibecast

Listen with intent.

Vibecast is a quiet, editorial podcast player for iPhone. Build small moods — morning, around the house, a workout, a wind down, deep work — and the show that fits the moment is one tap away.

What it does
  • Subscribe to any RSS podcast feed.
  • Group shows into vibes — small playlists that match how you listen.
  • One tap to start a vibe; episodes queue up in order.
  • Everything lives on your device. No account, no cloud sync, no tracking.
